Hal Auden Cumberbatch is a celebrity kid born to Benedict Cumberbatch and Sophie Hunter. His father is a famous British actor mainly known for his role as Dr. Strange in the Marvel Universe. Similarly, his mother, on the other hand, is a well-known theater and opera director.

He came into the limelight from the day he was born. So, there are a lot of fans and people who want to know more about him. Does he have any siblings? when is his birthday? where does he live? How is his lifestyle? There are a lot of questions but not too many answers.

Thus, today in this article we are going to cover all the facts and details about Hal Auden Cumberbatch. So, what are we waiting for, let's get dive in!

Who is Hal Auden Cumberbatch? Details Regarding Benedict Cumberbatch's Son

He was born on March 3rd, 2017 to his mother Sophie Hunter, and father Benedict Cumberbatch. His zodiac sign is Pisces. Similarly, talking about his age, Hal is five years old as of 2022.

Additionally, his parents gave him the name 'Hal Auden' where Hal is a nickname for Henry Harold and Harry; a Shakespearean character, and Auden is a name of British origin which accounts for 'old friend.'

Furthermore, the celebrity kid has English ancestry and is British by nationality.

Talking about his education, there is not much information available in that regard. But because of his background, we can say that he is studying in a prestigious school in his locality.

Hal Auden's Father and Mother are Both Renowned Personalities

The celebrity kid's parents are both famous. His father Benedict Timothy Carlton Cumberbatch aka Benedict Cumberbatch is an English actor. He is better known for his outstanding acting in Sherlock

from 2010 to 2017 where he played the lead character Sherlock Holmes. Similarly, He rose to fame
mainly for playing Dr. Strange in the Marvel Cinematic Universe.

The multi-talented actor has also won various awards during his long acting career. He has won the British Academy Television Award, Primetime Emmy Award, and Laurence Olivier Award. Along with his win, he has also been nominated for two Academy Awards, two British Academy Film Awards, and four Golden Globe Awards.

Similarly, his mother Sophie Hunter is a well-known English playwright, theatre director, and former actress and singer. She started her career and made her directorial debut in 2007, codirecting The Terrific Electric at the Barbican Pit. Along with that, she has also directed an Off-Off-Broadway (2010). Sophie directed Phaedra and The Turn of the Screw (2015).

Besides being a director, Hunter has also starred in a couple of tv series like Midsomer Murders (2004), Mumbai Calling (2007), and Torchwood (2009).

When did Hal Auden's Parents Meet, Are They Still Together?

Benedict Cumberbatch and Sophie Hunter reportedly met in 2009 on a set while filming Burlesque Fairytales. The couple first appeared together in a French Open in June of 2014. Later that year they shocked their fans when the pair announced their engagement in November 2014.

Talking about their marriage the two lovebirds exchanged vows on the 14th of February 2015 on Valentine's Day. The wedding took place at the Church of St. Peter and St. Paul in Mottistone, England.

The couple has been married since then and is enjoying their life together. They have also shared three sons since their marriage in 2015.

Hal Auden Has Two Brothers

He is the second eldest son and has two brothers. His elder brother was born Christopher Carlton Cumberbatch on June 1, 2015. He got the middle name 'Carlton' from his paternal grandfather. Similarly, the younger one is named Finn Cumberbatch born in 2019.

According to their parents, all three siblings enjoy each other's company and love to spend time together, thus having an inseparable bond.
